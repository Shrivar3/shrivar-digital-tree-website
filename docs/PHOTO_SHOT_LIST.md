# Photography shot list

The site already reserves these compositions and automatically uses each image when it exists. Export high-quality JPEGs in sRGB, keep the subject away from text-safe edges, and avoid heavy filters.

## `public/images/shrivar-environmental-portrait.jpg`

- Crop: vertical 4:5, minimum 1600 × 2000 px.
- Scene: Shrivar in a real working or university environment, three-quarter body, looking slightly away from camera.
- Light: directional window light with a cool room and one warm practical source.
- Wardrobe: dark navy, charcoal, or off-white; no loud logos.
- Background: enough architectural depth for the cyan branch overlay to remain legible.

## `public/images/shrivar-working-session.jpg`

- Crop: landscape 3:2, minimum 2400 × 1600 px.
- Scene: candid desk or whiteboard session with notebook, code, or research material genuinely in use.
- Light: soft side light; preserve screen and paper detail without staged neon lighting.
- Wardrobe: relaxed, neutral layers.
- Background: working texture is welcome, but remove private or client-sensitive information.

## `public/images/shrivar-life-energy.jpg`

- Crop: vertical 3:4, minimum 1800 × 2400 px.
- Scene: an energetic non-desk moment—walking in London, sport, or a candid conversation—that adds life beyond the CV.
- Light: late-afternoon natural light or clean overcast light.
- Wardrobe: personal and relaxed; avoid corporate portrait styling.
- Background: simple urban or green context with visible negative space.

Do not substitute AI-generated likenesses. The editorial branch-field fallback remains intentional until authentic photographs are available.
